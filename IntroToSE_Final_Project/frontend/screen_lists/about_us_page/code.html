Our Journey

How we grew from a garage project to a global platform.

2018

The Idea was Born

Frustrated with complex spreadsheets, our founders built the first version of Money Lover to simply track daily expenses.

2020

Hitting 1 Million Users

Without any major marketing, the community grew. We expanded the team and introduced bank syncing features.

2023

Going Global

Now supporting over 150 countries and 30+ currencies. Money Lover becomes the go-to app for travelers and expats.
